Projects

  • PERFORMANCE IMPROVEMENT OF PHOTO VOLTAIC FED SERIES ACTIVE POWER FILTERS FOR DISTRIBUTED GENERATION

    • This paper deals with the performance of Series Active Power Filter (SAPF) for the power quality improvement based on the distributed generation. The SAPF has the ability to mitigate the voltage related problems in the distribution system. The real power supply is given by the PV system (Distributed Generation) for the compensation of the voltage related problems. The control strategy is achieved through the Synchronous Reference Frame Theory for the generation of the reference voltage which is compared with constant voltage for pulse generation using hysteresis band PWM. The SAPF has the better performance in the elimination of the voltage related problems in the distribution side. The performance of SAPF is modeled and simulated using MATLAB / SIMULINK

  • TRAIN ACCIDENT PREVENTION SYSTEM

    • The increasing reliance on rail transportation demands a robust and comprehensive Train Accident Prevention System (TAPS) to ensure the safety of passengers, crew, and the public. This paper outlines a holistic approach to TAPS, integrating advanced technologies and safety measures to mitigate potential risks. Key components include Automatic Train Control (ATC) systems employing sensors and communication networks for real-time monitoring and control. Positive Train Control (PTC) ensures collision avoidance and overspeed derailment prevention through GPS, wireless communication, and onboard control systems. Advanced signaling, infrastructure monitoring, and improved railroad crossing safety enhance overall safety. Human factors, cybersecurity, and continuous data analysis contribute to the efficacy of the system. Collaboration among stakeholders and a commitment to ongoing maintenance and improvement are essential for the successful implementation of TAPS in the dynamic landscape of rail transportation.

  • ELECTRIC BICYCLE

    • The Electric Bicycle Project aims to design, develop, and implement an innovative electric bicycle (e-bike) that combines sustainable transportation with cutting-edge technology. The e-bike is designed to comply with regulatory standards, promoting safety and accessibility for riders of various skill levels.
